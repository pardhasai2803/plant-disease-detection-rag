The plant requires a well-draining soil with a pH between 6.0 and 6.8, and a consistent supply of essential nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

They require full sun, with at least 6 hours of direct sunlight per day, and consistent moisture, with about 1-2 inches of water per week. Well-draining soil and adequate air circulation also contribute to a healthy plant.
